1. How to Make Homemade Popcorn in the Microwave
Easy Microwave Popcorn This microwave hack will give you the best popcorn and it's so easy! Use these tips and tricks to have movie night snacks galore with all kinds of flavor options!
This method for making homemade popcorn in the microwave comes from recipe creator ACONTORER who says, "Believe it or not, you can make your own delicious,
low-fat microwave popcorn using standard popping corn and a brown paper lunch bag. It works perfectly.
" Save money without sacrificing flavor or convenience by microwaving your own popcorn from a brown paper bag.

Cook in the microwave on high for 2 Â½ to 3 minutes, or until you hear pauses of about 2 seconds between pops. Carefully open the bag to avoid steam, and pour into a serving bowl.
